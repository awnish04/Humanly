## HOW IT WORKS NOW:

1. **Paste or upload your text** in the input panel
2. **Click "Check for AI"** to see if it's AI-generated
3. AI detection panel shows:
   - AI percentage (0-100%)
   - Cross-check with 8 detectors
   - Full report with breakdown
4. **Click "Humanize AI"** to rewrite the text
5. **Copy the humanized output** from the right panel
